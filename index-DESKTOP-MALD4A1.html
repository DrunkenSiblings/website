Welcome to our bookclub

I wanted to start spending time with my siblings. So I thought we could do a podcast while playing boardgames. As it turned out we are too competitive to be talkative while trying to win, win, WIN!! So I came up with the idea to start an online book club. So here we are now, not only have my sisters joined me, our mother and a cousin (Gina) will be joining us as often as they can.

I think that we are a lot more entertaining (at the very least more talkative), now that we are not trying to beat each other in a game. All we want to do is have fun discussing books, having a few beers/wine.

You will find the latest episode down below. We also have the backlog listed on our archive page. Also you can find a list of upcoming books on the right hand side of the page. Thank you for visiting our website. Please subscribe to our feed to keep up to date.

The latest episode

To stop her sadistic grandfather, Saul, and his band of time travelers from rewriting history, Kate must race to retrieve the CHRONOS keys before they fall into the Cyrists' hands. If she jumps back in time and pulls the wrong key one that might tip off the Cyrists to her strategy her whole plan could come crashing down, jeopardizing the future of millions of innocent people. Kate's only ally is Kiernan, who also carries the time-traveling gene. But their growing bond threatens everything Kate is trying to rebuild with Trey, her boyfriend who can't remember the relationship she can't forget.

As evidence of Saul's twisted mind builds, Kate's missions become more complex, blurring the line between good and evil. Which of the people Saul plans to sacrifice in the past can she and Kiernan save without risking their ultimate goal or their own lives?
